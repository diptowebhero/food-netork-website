About Me

Hi, I’m Anna Bloomfield I have one fantastic husband, two gorgeous children, three noisy chickens and the most adorable, sweet, rescue black lab. I’m lucky enough to live in a great village and have a job I actually enjoy.have always loved food and cooking. Some of my earliest memories are of poring over cookbooks then making pudding on a Sunday with Dad. My Mum is a product of the 1970’s health food movement and I was brought up eating things my friends had never even heard of.
